Why Work With Us

We're highly motivated, a lot of fun, very quirky and most importantly, passionate about solving healthcare's messiest problems with new technologies. We value diversity, curiosity and a passion for learning. The team has a lot of degrees, ex-athletes and even a few professional musicians!

Meet Our Leadership Team

With a combined 65+ years of healthcare and technology experience, were dedicated to solving todays most challenging problems.

Matt Brandt

CEO and Founder

Matt Brandt

CEO and Founder

  • Matt founded Casechek when he couldnt find a hospital that was happy with how they managed vendors and vendor inventory. Thanks to loyal customers and their amazing feedback, what started as simple software solution in December of 2014, is now a comprehensive platform and service used by leading institutions and healthcare systems across the country.

    Prior to starting Casechek, Matt spent most of his career in sales and marketing roles for healthcare startups and medical device companies. He grew up in his favorite city, Toronto, Canada and somehow made his way to Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. While at Miami, Matt played football and graduated with a double major in Manufacturing Engineering and Engineering Management. Not to be outdone by his studious parents, Matt went back to school and earned his MBA from Northwestern Universitys Kellogg School of Management. Matt lives in Chicagos West Loop with his amazing wife Margo, their four over-active children and Jost.

Heidi Graham

Heidi Graham

Chief Operating Officer

Heidi is a healthcare tech leader and organization builder who thrives on working at the intersection of people, process and technology. Heidi began her career at Stryker in quality engineering and value based care. Casechek is on a mission to innovate the implant supply chain and bring greater transparency to the cost of patient care. As aData Analyst - Client Outcomesat Casechek, you will be at the forefront of data-driven decision-making and innovation. You'll ensure the integrity of our analytics pipelines through cutting-edge automation and best practices. Your insights will empower sales prospects, clients, and internal stakeholders, making a tangible difference in our business and beyond. Join us and be a pivotal force in transforming data into actionable strategies that fuel growth and success!
