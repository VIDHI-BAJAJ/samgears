About Us

Established in 1996, Sam Gears (India) Pvt. Ltd has become a prominent manufacturer and exporter of transmission gears and shafts for a variety of tractors, including Massey Ferguson, Zetor, Ford, Romania UTB 650, and Mahindra. In addition to transmission components, the company also produces tractor spare parts and automotive hydraulic filters, and is capable of undertaking turnkey projects within the industry. Their manufacturing processes are supported by hi-tech facilities and sophisticated machinery, allowing for efficient production operations such as blank operation, gear cutting, heat treatment, metallurgy, and grinding. This advanced infrastructure ensures the delivery of high-quality products by maximizing resource utilization.

Under the astute leadership of Mr. Kamal Mehta, the company has thrived in the competitive automotive sector. The team, composed of skilled professionals including automobile engineers, designers, production engineers, quality inspectors, administrative officers, customer care executives, and semi-skilled workers, works in harmony to meet and exceed customer expectations. Their proficiency with the latest process technologies and machinery has been instrumental in achieving operational excellence and timely delivery of products.

Sam Gears (India) Pvt. Ltd has made significant strides in international markets, bolstered by the cost competitiveness of their products. The company’s broad product range encompasses transmission gears and shafts for various tractor models, including Massey Ferguson, Ford, Zetor 2011/2511, HMT Zetor 5911, UTB 650, and Mahindra. They also provide an array of tractor spare parts such as rear axles, spindles, hydraulic pumps, and automotive hydraulic filters. These components are manufactured using high-grade raw materials and are subject to stringent quality tests to ensure they meet the exact specifications of their clients.

The success of Sam Gears (India) Pvt. Ltd can be attributed to the dedication and expertise of their team. The professionals work closely together to ensure that customer requirements are fulfilled on or before the scheduled deadlines. Their deep understanding of process technologies and the use of sophisticated machines enable them to maintain high standards of quality and efficiency in production. This commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has established Sam Gears (India) Pvt. Ltd as a trusted name in the manufacturing and export of transmission gears, shafts, and related components for tractors.
