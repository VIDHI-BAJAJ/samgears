VENDOR CHALLENGES

Every month, Dublin Methodist found itself facing several vendor-related issues:

1. High lost instrument spend
2. Disproportionate time spent managing vendors
3. Overtime pay for SPD staff to process vendor trays
4. Low vendor compliance with established processes

Dublin lacked the necessary resources to enforce vendor compliance. Dublin needed a partner with established technology and best practices to help them focus on more important clinical work.

RESULTS WITH CASECHEK

Dublin partnered with Casechek in the Spring of 2017 and experienced significant improvement across the board. Here are the results from June 2017 to May 2018:
